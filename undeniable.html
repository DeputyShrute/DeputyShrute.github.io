Album Info:

Young Sinatra: Undeniable is the third mixtape by American rapper Logic. It was released as a free online download by Visionary Music Group on April 30, 2012 and serves as the second part of the Young Sinatra mixtape trilogy, following Young Sinatra and preceding Young Sinatra: Welcome to Forever. The mixtape has been downloaded over 820,000 times on DatPiff as of July 2017. This also was the project that helped skyrocket his career because of the song "The Spotlight" which the famous internet group FaZe used in a montage.
